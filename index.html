Yellowfields

Yellowfields is a collaborative publication which brings together and highlights the new work of emerging female artists. It is Yellowfields ethos to work with artists who want to change how we see the world. They do this through testing ideas, challenging societal views, incorporate science and politics, take care of the world and believe their work has a voice.
