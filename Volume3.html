Intuitive Geometries: Women Making Contemporary Sculpture

This third and final volume of yellowfields print Intuitive Geometries: Women Making Contemporary Sculpture features artist texts;

  • Slow Gin, The Foot Cage / Averaging by Olivia Bax,
  • A series of texts by Solveig Settemsdal,
  • Acts of Care: A Manifesto by Linda Brothwell,
  • Syracuse by Nika Neelova
  • Essay Intuitive Geometries: Women Making Contemporary Sculpture by curator and art historian Jennifer Dudley.

    • Connected through the making of sculpture, this critical group’s work delve into ideas around material and process, space, ergonomics, and responsibility.
