Hello

Yellowfields is an independent project investigating critical ideas through the writing of emerging womxn artists. Across three thematic publications, featured artist texts sit alongside a curatorial or art historical essay which highlights overarching ideas. The project aims to generate critical impact through highlighting environmental and socio-political issues within art practice.

This project is curated by Georgia Hall and was kindly supported by Ganes Trust, Arts Council England and Fenner Paper.
