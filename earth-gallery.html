Earth Art Fellowship Initiative

Earth Art, developed in 2016, is an ongoing collaboration between artists based in the South West and the staff and students from the School of Earth Sciences as well as the wider academic community at the University of Bristol. Find out more here

´The idea of developing the fellowship and the earth gallery as the university was not just about public engagement but a way to explore a different angle for the scientists; maybe changing how they see what they are doing to generate a more balanced outlook, taking into account the social aspects not just the economical’

    Rodney Harris, artist and member of the Earth Art arts council.

Supporting this initiative as an associate curator, I have produced publications of the projects as an opportunity to reflect, share and document the fellowship, public event and exhibition of the selected artist, exploring the collaboration with the School of Earth Sciences at the University of Bristol.
